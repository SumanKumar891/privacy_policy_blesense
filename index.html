Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

BLE Sense is a mobile application that helps you scan, connect, and interact with Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Bluetooth Device Information

  • Device names and identifiers
  • MAC addresses
  • Signal strength (RSSI)
  • Available services

Location Data

  • Required on Android 6.0+ for Bluetooth scanning
  • Used only for BLE device discovery
  • Not stored or shared

Account Information (Optional)

  • Email address (for login functionality)
  • Securely hashed passwords

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Device Discovery: To find and connect to nearby BLE devices
  • Data Display: To show sensor readings and device information
  • User Authentication: To provide secure login (if you choose to create an account)
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and enhance performance

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All BLE data is processed locally on your device
  • No personal information is shared with third parties
  • Firebase is used only for basic authentication and app functionality
  • Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for:

  • User authentication (login/signup)
  • Basic app functionality
  • No data is shared beyond these essential services

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and data at any time
  • Disable location permissions in your device settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
