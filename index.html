Privacy Policy for BLE Sense

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

BLE Sense is a mobile application designed to scan, connect, and interact with Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information to provide core functionality:

  • Bluetooth Device Information: Device names, MAC addresses, signal strength (RSSI), service UUIDs
  • Location Data (If Required): Required on Android 6.0+ for BLE scanning (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION or ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION)
  • Authentication Data (If Using Login): Email (if registered), password (securely hashed)
  • App Usage Data (Optional): Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics), screen interactions

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To discover and connect to BLE devices
  • To display sensor data from connected devices
  • To improve app performance (via crash reports)
  • To authenticate users (if applicable)
